01 · Inputs

Three inputs.
One verdict.

Every Plumbline verdict is the composition of three things — the plans the bank pinned to the loan, the finance plan that prices them, and the imagery that proves them. Each arrives on its own cadence.
01
PDF · slow

Construction plans

Architectural, structural, electrical, plumbing drawings. Agent 1 classifies pages; Agent 2 extracts a discipline-shaped format that photos can later match.

Once at loan origination · Versioned on CO
02
G703 · slow

Finance plan

Schedule of Values with milestones tied to required completion per discipline. Agent 3 parses line items + draws. Each milestone gates a dollar release.

Once at loan origination · Amended on CO
03
IMG · live

Jobsite imagery

Phone photos from the builder, arriving monthly or continuously. Agent 5 gates quality; Agent 6 converts each photo into the plan's format for diffing.

Monthly per draw · Continuous between
